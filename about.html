BuildMyPC

Welcome to BuildMyPC, your one-stop destination for crafting a PC that caters to your unique needs and budget. Our platform offers an extensive range of components, giving you the freedom to customize your PC with precision.

Our Mission

Our mission at BuildMyPC is straightforward – to assist and empower individuals in building their dream PCs. Through our comprehensive platform, we aim to simplify and streamline the process of PC building, eliminating the hassles of compatibility checks and ensuring a seamless experience.

Our Story

Founded in 2019 by Sunny Kumar and Sunny Bundel, both computer science enthusiasts, BuildMyPC was born out of a desire to simplify the process of custom PC building. Over the years, we have grown exponentially and take pride in offering the latest PC components to our users.

Who Are We ?

The editorial team at BuildMyPC brings an extensive background in science and a deep understanding of PC parts. Our collective expertise ensures that we provide accurate and reliable information to our users, helping them make informed decisions about their PC components.

Our Responsibility

As an affiliate of Amazon, we generate revenue through affiliate marketing which helps us maintain our platform free for our users. We ensure transparency in all our practices and are committed to maintaining the highest levels of ethical standards.

Why Choose Us ?

What sets BuildMyPC apart is our unique approach towards helping users build their PCs. We allow you to choose parts according to your needs and budget, ensuring compatibility with all components you select. This unique value proposition makes us a preferred choice for PC enthusiasts.

Connect With Us

While we currently do not offer engagement options such as newsletters and social media, we are continually evolving and look forward to introducing more features on our platform.
